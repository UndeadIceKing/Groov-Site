What's inside

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Groov is designed around one idea: making it as easy as possible to show up every day.

✅

Daily & Volume Habits

Track simple check-off habits or volume-based ones like glasses of water or minutes of exercise.

🔥

Streaks & Perfect Days

Build momentum with daily streaks. See your perfect days mount up as consistency becomes a habit itself.

Challenges

Set up to 3 concurrent challenges tied to your habits. Earn tiered completions based on your performance.

Progress Tracking

Weekly bar charts and a 7/30/90-day heatmap give you an honest picture of how you're really doing.

Smart Reminders

Set custom notification times per habit so the right nudge arrives exactly when you need it.

Cloud Sync

Your habits follow you. Everything syncs securely to the cloud so you never lose your progress.

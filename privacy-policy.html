1 Information We Collect

Groov collects only what it needs to provide the service. We do not sell your data and do not use advertising networks.

Data Type Examples Purpose
Account credentials Email address, hashed password Authentication & account access
Habit & activity data Habit names, completion logs, streaks, challenge progress, daily snapshots Core app functionality & cloud sync
App settings Theme preference, notification preferences, volume/duration targets Personalisation & syncing across sessions
Push notification tokens Device push token (Expo) Delivering habit reminders you configure

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera/photos, payment information, or any government-issued identifiers.

2 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Create and authenticate your account
  • Sync your habits, completions, challenges, and settings across sessions
  • Send push notifications for habit reminders you have enabled
  • Send occasional in-app or push notifications about new features or important service updates
  • Maintain streaks, challenge progress, and historical snapshots

We do not use your data to train machine learning models, build advertising profiles, or infer sensitive characteristics about you.

3 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase, a cloud database platform with row-level security (RLS) enforced at the database level. This means your records are only accessible to your authenticated account. No other user can read or write your data.

  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • We do not store passwords in plaintext; authentication is handled via Supabase's secure auth system.
Row-level security: Every table in our database enforces a policy that restricts all reads and writes to the authenticated user who owns that data.

4 Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own purposes. Your data may only be disclosed in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Supabase processes and stores your data on our behalf, under their own privacy and security commitments.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users or the public.
  • Business transfer: If the app is acquired or merged, your data may transfer to the new owner under equivalent privacy protections.

5 Push Notifications

Groov uses push notifications for two purposes:

  • Habit reminders: Scheduled at times you configure per habit. You can disable these individually in the app's Settings screen or by turning off notifications for Groov in your device's iOS Settings.
  • App updates & announcements: Occasional messages about significant new features or service changes. You can opt out of these at any time in the app's Settings screen.

We use Expo's push notification service to deliver notifications to your device. Your push token is stored in our database solely for this delivery purpose.

6 Children's Privacy

Groov is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at beastlyiceking@gmail.com and we will delete the account promptly.

7 Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information (habit names and settings can be edited directly in the app)
  • Reset your password at any time via the Settings screen in the app or the "Forgot password?" link on the login screen
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time via the Settings screen in the app (see Section 8)
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via iOS Settings or in-app Settings
  • Export a copy of your data by contacting us at beastlyiceking@gmail.com

8 Deleting Your Account & Data

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time using either method below.

Option 1 — Delete in-app (immediate): Open Groov, go to Settings → Delete Account, and confirm. Your account and all data are deleted instantly.
Option 2 — Email request: Email beastlyiceking@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and the email address associated with your account. We will complete the deletion within 30 days and confirm via email.

Upon deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your account credentials and email address
  • All habits, completions, streaks, and challenge history
  • App settings and preferences
  • Push notification tokens
  • Daily snapshots and progress data

Deletion is irreversible. We do not retain backups of deleted accounts beyond our standard 30-day database backup window, after which all traces are gone.

9 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: You may request details about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information (see Section 8).
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell personal information, so this right does not apply, but you may still contact us to confirm.

To exercise your California rights, contact us at beastlyiceking@gmail.com.

10 Changes to This Policy

For significant changes, we will notify you via push notification or email. Your continued use of Groov after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
